Frequently Asked Questions

4Phases FAQ
What is 4Phases?

4Phases is an AI platform designed for Clinical Research Organizations (CROs) and sites to automate patient enrollment and management for clinical trials. It replaces traditional human patient enrollment teams with a scalable, 24/7 AI system that functions like 100 employees, handling interactions via voice, text, and email. Our AI, named Helly, uses a humanized voice to qualify patients, gather detailed health records, and push everything back to your EHR system seamlessly.

How is 4Phases HIPPA compliant?

4Phases ensures HIPAA compliance through its zero data storage architecture, functioning solely as a secure, real-time interface rather than a data warehouse. Upon setup, CROs connect their EHR database via a secure API, providing endpoint URLs and authentication keys. Patient data is pulled in real-time only during queries (e.g., prescreening), processed by the Helly AI on a separate service, and immediately pushed back to the EHR without ever being stored on 4Phases servers.

How does 4Phases streamline patient recruitment in clinical trials?

4Phases addresses the fact that 86% of clinical trials fail to meet recruitment goals by automating the entire process, saving time and money that would otherwise go to building and maintaining a sales team—or even having licensed doctors handle cold calls. Our AI prescreens patients against trial protocols in real-time from your EHR, identifies matches with high accuracy, and qualifies them through personalized interactions. Even if a patient doesn't qualify, Helly collects expanded medical records to enrich your database for future trials, turning every interaction into value and potentially replacing the cost of an entire outbound team with the price of one employee.

What sets 4Phases apart from other tools?

Unlike generic solutions that rely on GPT wrappers or human callers, 4Phases uses a custom proprietary AI hosted on our servers for superior performance and valuation potential. Helly's humanized, non-robotic voice ensures natural conversations. It scales effortlessly to act as a full sales force, integrates directly with EHR systems, and includes advanced features like booking Ubers for patient visits or scheduling follow-ups, making enrollment faster, more efficient, and far more cost-effective than human-led efforts.
